DAY 1


Keeping it in mind IETE Students Forum, BIT Sindri, comes with another prodigious webinar on Cyber security. This amazing workshop was organized by IETE students Forum on 8th of October 2021 in the evening 6 p.m.


The session was live on our YouTube channel and is led by our honorable speaker Mr. Vanshit Malhotra. Vanshit Malhotra is one of the well known Cyber Security Experts in India; has been a Cyber Security Researcher for more than 8 years. Vanshit is a Cyber Security Specialist, with his assignments pointed towards reducing the state of Insecurity of Information. He is a sought after public Speaker and Cyber Security Researcher, presenting his research at many international security conferences Vanshit Malhotra is Founder of HackDoor India - An Advanced Cyber Security Training Company.


Cybersecurity professionals are wanted in every organization due to their knowledge in data security. The availability of skilled professionals who can tackle the cybersecurity issue is significantly less in India. Having a college degree is the right way of starting a career in cybersecurity, but it is not the only way. 43% of cybersecurity professionals are certification holders, who got training besides the college degree.

To shine in the cybersecurity career, we recommend you to get some practical training. The pragmatic cybersecurity world will assist you to develop a couple of soft skills required if choosing a career in cybersecurity. Some of the skills are-:

  1. Problem solver
  2. Enthusiast
  3. Quick learner
  4. Teacher

To begin, one must start with an entry-level job within the field and equip themselves with adequate training and knowledge.The following are the varied job roles to start a career in cybersecurity -

  1. Security Architect
  2. Security Consultant
  3. Penetration Tester
  4. Cryptographer
  5. Security Engineer
  6. Security Analyst

Overall this workshop reached to a successful status with an enormous response and participation. The appealing session ended with an electrifying quiz round where winner was given amazing prizes.



DAY 2


After the successful conduction of the 1st webinar of our webinar series we were very excited to move forward and announce the second webinar on the topic Security Intelligence. And the guest speaker for this session is Dinesh Paranthagan.


Dinesh Paranthagan is the CEO & Founder of Hackup Technology, Coimbatore. He has an experience for about 8 years in managing and administrating in the domain of Cyber security and Ethical Hacking. Hackup Technology is in collaboration with 54 colleges and 7 MNC's in and around Tamil Nadu. It also offers the Information Security /services, VAPT, Risk Audit and Public compliance solution to it's customers and clients. And he taught us what actually security intelligence is and what are its benifits like: The term Security Intelligence describes the practice of collecting, standardizing and analyzing data that is generated by networks, applications, and other IT infrastructure in real-time, and the use of that information to assess and improve an organization's security posture. It Supported the consumption of threat intelligence, it's divided into four differing types. they're specifically strategic threat intelligence , tactical threat intelligence , operational threat intelligence , and technical threat intelligence


Security Intelligence aids both regulatory and internal policy compliance by logging and proactively monitoring diverse information across the enterprise in real time, providing accountability, transparency and measurability.


After the successful session there was an interesting quiz in which a good amount of parricipants participated and also praised the way we condudted the session and the quiz.



DAY 3


After the blooming conduction of the 1st & 2nd webinar, we were now very thrilled to move forward to intend to make people more cognizant of security via our third webinar on the topic SQL Injection, and the guest speaker for this session is Riyaz Rafi Ahmed.


Mr. Riyaz Rafi Ahmed is a highly motivated and self-driven Cybersecurity Professional & Public Speaker with more than 3 years of experience in Architecting Defensive Solutions, DevSecOps, and Managing Information Security at Hippo Video. He is also active in the Cybersecurity community as a speaker and has been invited to multiple talks related to Online Privacy, Personal security, and has conducted Security awareness training for a wide range of audiences from students to seasoned industry professionals. Cybersecurity is a subject that requires logic, knowledge, thought, and commitment. It can be applied or research-based. It is a true leveler for all to enter, be successful, and lead the future of Cyber Security.


He taught us that SQL is a common attack vector that uses malicious SQL code for backend database manipulation to access information that was not intended to be displayed. This information may include any number of items, including sensitive company data, user lists, or private customer details. And many more things about SQL.
